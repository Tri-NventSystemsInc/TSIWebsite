About Us

Tri-Nvent is a software development company based in Dumaguete City, Philippines which uses the recent state-of-the-art technology for the Enterprise in the areas of Java(Java EE and SE), vb.net, Android technology and many others. We specialize in building custom applications to various type of industries. We are expert in data migration and enterprise application integration, web designs and development, and Wireless Network Designs and Implementations. The co-founders have merged their expertise and experience in software development and financial accounting for the commitment of providing quality products and services.

History

Tri-nvent System's, Inc. was founded on September 2009 by three exceptional individuals, Ramil D. Repe, a certified public accountant, Rey D. Repe and Voltaire F. Picardo, both experts in Systems Administration, and Software Development and Designs.

TSI was formed from humble beginnings. The primary goal was to create job opportunities to local java programmers who are fresh graduates from Universities in Dumaguete City, Philippines.

It is our passion to provide software related products and services to companies enabling them to compete in their respective line of industries for a very reasonable cost.

The company has started out in outsourcing software development projects from one of the prestigious software development company in the Philippines as lone client. For its competitive edge solutions, Tri-Nvent has gained widespread reputation as surging software development company with growing number of clients from various industry.

With the incorporator's commitment for innovation and success in its line of business, TSI expanded its services by accepting outsourcing projects worldwide.
